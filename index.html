About Caliber Manufacturing

Located in Carlos, Minnesota, Caliber Manufacturing, Inc. has been at the forefront of precision manufacturing since our establishment. Our state-of-the-art facility at 5188 Co Rd 13 NE is equipped with cutting-edge technology and staffed by highly skilled professionals who are committed to delivering excellence in every project.

We specialize in high-precision manufacturing services for the automotive industry, providing both custom fabrication and repairs. Our commitment to quality and efficiency has made us a trusted partner for businesses seeking reliable manufacturing solutions. From prototype development to full-scale production, we maintain the highest standards of precision and quality control.

Our team combines decades of experience with modern manufacturing techniques to deliver superior results. Whether you need custom automotive parts, precision machining, or comprehensive manufacturing solutions, we have the expertise and capabilities to meet your specific requirements while maintaining strict quality standards and timely delivery.
